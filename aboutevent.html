Conference Themes

  • Use of new and alternate materials and technologies in construction of Rural Road (Low Volume Roads)
  • Design of sustainable Rural Roads (Low Volume Roads)
  • Low cost surfacing for Rural Roads
  • Climate Resilient Rural Road Infrastructure
  • Latest Technologies in Bridge Construction on Rural Roads
  • Design and Construction of Rural Roads in hilly terrain/cyclone prone areas
  • Asset Management, Innovations in Maintainance and Rehabilitation Strategies for Rural Roads (Low Volume Roads) and International Best Practices
  • Design and Construction of low volume roads in snowbound areas

Technical Exhibition

The Conference will provide a platform to showcase the latest technologies,new materials,equipment,road construction machinary, road safety materials, automatic traffic counters cum classifiers, quality control equipment related to rural road construction, maintenance and asset management.

Technical Institutions, Research Institutions, Government Officials, Road Construction Agencies, Consultants, Experts, Equipment Manufacturers,etc. are cordially invited to participate in the three day event.
